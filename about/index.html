Why did we create GoggleTools

GoggleTools was created to provide a cleaner, faster, and more user-friendly alternative to traditional citation tools. Many existing platforms are slow, outdated, and require accounts. This project focuses on simplicity, performance, and accessibility for all students and everyone.
